Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • White Vinegar
  • Bay Leaves
  • Peppercorns
  • Onions
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Potato (optional)
  • Boiled Egg (optional)
  • Pineapple (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Combine your desired meat, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour.
  2. Step 2: Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes.
  3. Step 3: Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Step 4: Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Step 5: Add vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Add salt to taste and other optional ingredients.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
