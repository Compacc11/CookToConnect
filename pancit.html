PANCIT BIHON

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pancit bihon (Rice Noodles)
  • 1/2 lb. pork, cut into thin slices
  • 1/2 lb. chicken, cooked, deboned, and sliced
  • 1/8 lb. snow peas
  • 1 cup carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 small cabbage, chopped
  • 1 cup celery leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chicken powder
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 to 4 cups water
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large wok. Saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add pork and chicken, cooking for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in chicken powder and water, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add carrots, snow peas, cabbage, and celery leaves; simmer for a few more minutes.
  5. Remove all ingredients from the pot, leaving the liquid, and set aside.
  6. Add soy sauce to the pot and stir well.
  7. Add pancit bihon (soaked for 10 minutes), and cook until liquid evaporates.
  8. Return vegetables and meat to the pot and simmer for a minute or two.
  9. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
