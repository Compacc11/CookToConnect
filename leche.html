LECHE FLAN

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces eggs
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Separate the yolk from the egg whites, using only the yolks.
  2. Step 2: Place the egg yolks in a large bowl and beat them with a fork or egg beater.
  3. Step 3: Add the condensed milk and mix thoroughly.
  4. Step 4: Pour in the fresh milk and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  5. Step 5: Heat the mold (llanera) on low heat and add the granulated sugar.
  6. Step 6: Stir the sugar in the mold until it turns into a light brown caramel.
  7. Step 7: Spread the caramel evenly on the flat surface of the mold and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Pour the egg yolk and milk mixture over the caramel in the mold.
  9. Step 9: Cover the mold with aluminum foil.
  10. Step 10: Steam the mold with the mixture for 30 to 35 minutes.
  11. Step 11: Allow it to cool, then refrigerate.
  12. Step 12: Serve as dessert and enjoy!
