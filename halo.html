HALO-HALO

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened white beans
  • 4 cups shaved ice (from about 6 cups of ice cubes)
  • 1 large banana, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup jackfruit, drained and sliced
  • 1/4 cup macapuno strips, drained
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco, drained
  • 1/4 cup red palm seeds (Kaong), drained
  • 1/4 cup green palm seeds (Kaong), drained
  • 1 cup evaporated milk, divided
  • 1/2 cup Cap'n Crunch with Crunch Berries cereal
  • 2 scoops ube ice cream (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients and two tall parfait glasses.
  2. Place half of the sweetened red and white beans at the bottom of each glass.
  3. Process half the ice cubes until they resemble snow, and scoop about half a cup of shaved ice over the beans in each glass.
  4. Add banana and jackfruit slices, followed by another half cup of shaved ice. Then add macapuno strips and nata de coco.
  5. Process the remaining ice cubes, then add another half cup of shaved ice on top of each glass.
  6. Layer with red and green palm seeds, then top with a final half cup of shaved ice, doming it slightly.
  7. Pour 1/2 cup evaporated milk on top of each parfait and add a scoop of ube ice cream.
  8. Top with Cap'n Crunch cereal and enjoy immediately with a long spoon for mixing!
